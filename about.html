About the Marlboro Mustangs

The Marlboro Mustangs Track Team has a long history of excellence. Established in 1950, our athletes have consistently competed at the state level, earning numerous championships and records.

Our mission is to foster teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship both on and off the track. Whether you’re a sprinter, distance runner, or field event athlete, there’s a place for you in our Mustang family.
